Sticky Footer

Usage:

Create a static footer on your web page. No matter how much content you have, footer will always be at the bottom of your page. Just add class .footer footer-center height-100.

There is an option fo you footer text to be on right (footer-right) or left (footer-left) as well.

Adjust your height with nessasy class: height-10, height-20, height-30, height-40, height-50, height-60, height-70, height-80, height-90, height-100

Add the code:

 <div class="footer footer-center height-100"> 
                <p>Custom text</p> 
</div>
